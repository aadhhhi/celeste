About CELESTE

Learn more about the history and purpose of our tournament

Where Football Dreams Begin

Welcome to CELESTE 2025, the most anticipated football championship at Memunda Higher Secondary School. This annual event brings together talented students from all grades, fostering a spirit of competition, teamwork, and sportsmanship.

Our tournament is designed to be more than just a game; it's a celebration of athletic prowess and school spirit. CELESTE provides a platform for young athletes to showcase their skills, build friendships, and create lasting memories.
